FAQ

  • How do I play Candy Blitz?
    Match 3 or more candies of the same type to clear them and earn points. Use boosters and complete level objectives to advance.
  • Is the game free?
    Yes! Candy Blitz is free to download and play. Optional in-game purchases are available.
  • Can I play offline?
    Absolutely — many features are accessible without an internet connection.
  • How do I save my progress?
    Link your game to your Google Play account to back up and sync progress.
  • What devices are supported?
    Most modern Android phones and tablets. iOS version may be available soon.
  • How often are new levels added?
    Every month! Stay tuned for special events and candy-themed updates.
